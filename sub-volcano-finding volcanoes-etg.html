Extra-terrestrial giants!

Volcanoes are not only found on Earth. They're also on other planets! The biggest volcano we know about in our solar system is on the planet Mars. It's called Olympus Mons. This monster is over 20 kilometres high and 600 kilometres wide. That's about five times the size of the Big Island of Hawaii! Don't forget that if you want to visit Olympus Mons, you'll need to do some astronaut training first!

Olympus Mons is perhaps about to lose top spot in the giant volcano stakes. This is because scientists have recently found what looks to be a single, huge, old volcano underwater in the north-west Pacific Ocean that is bigger in area! This volcano is called the Tamu Massif. More work needs to be done to fully understand this watery Earth giant but Olympus Mons better watch out!
