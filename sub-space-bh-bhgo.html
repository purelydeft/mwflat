Where do Black Holes Go?

 

Where do black holes go? If you keep feeding them with gas and dust from nearby stars, they just keep getting bigger and bigger. But what if you just leave them alone?

The astrophysicist Stephen Hawking has proposed that black holes can very gradually leak energy, and thus lose mass. This process, called Hawking radiation, would result in black holes evaporating away.

The smallest black holes would disappear almost instantly, while black holes the mass of the Sun would survive for a very long time. Not a billion years, not a trillion years, but far far longer!
