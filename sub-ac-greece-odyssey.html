The Odyssey

When Odysseus left Troy after the end of war, his ship was hit by a storm and blown off course. It took him ten years to reach his home in Ithaca!

On his way home, Odysseus had many strange and terrifying adventures. He was captured by the terrible monster Cyclops, and was only able to escape by using his powers of trickery!
